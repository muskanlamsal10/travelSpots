Tybee Island, Georgia






About Tybee Island



Tybee Island is a small island located near Savannah, Georgia popular for its historic values. It consists of 19th century old gun batteries and Light Station and Museum which is a major attraction for tourists. It has nature-friendly atmosphere with sandy beaches that is perfect for swimming, kayaking, fishing sunbathing and other water sports. People who love warm weather can visit this place from April through late May and also September to early November. It is also famous for its local events like Beach Bum Parade, which is a water gun fight festival, so get ready.

Things to do

Dolphins sightseeing, air-boat tours, Tybee Island Light Station and Museum, Tybee Pier and Pavilion, Tybee Post Theater, paddle boarding, kayaking, Fort Pulaski National Monument, Cockspur Island Lighthouse, Beach Bum Parade and Pirate Fest Parade.
