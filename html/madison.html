Madison,Connecticut






About Madison



Madison, Connecticut is a town in the southeastern corner of New Haven County, populating a central area on Connecticut's Long Island Sound shoreline. Madison is famous for its beautiful beaches, its vibrant culture, and its excellent schools. The best time of the year to visit Madison for ideal weather is from early May to mid July.
