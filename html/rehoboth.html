Rehoboth Beach, Delaware






About Rehoboth Beach



Rehoboth Beach is an Atlantic Coast town which is the most visited place in Delaware. Rehoboth Beach is a famous family sea shore destination on the East Coast. With 30 miles of coastline, sports fans can entertain themselves with kayaking, paddle boarding, cruising, and surfing, while others can go for a stroll along the manicured strolling trails and appreciate the view. The best time of the year to go on a vacation at the Rehoboth beach is between May and August as it sparkles the most in the summer months where the visitors can take full advantage of the boardwalk and the beach. It is a perfect place for summer vacation.
