Galena, Illinois






About Galena



Galena is a town of historical architecture form 19th century with many sights and seeing in Illinois. It offers well-preserved antique buildings like the 1826 Dowling House and home of the Civil War general and also the 18th president of United States, Ulysses S. Grant. Galena attracts thousands of tourists each year through its history, nature, architecture and beautiful mountain resorts famous for skiing. There is something to do for everyone, you can either go hiking, riding, skiing, kayaking, or sight-seeing, wildlife visiting, river walk, watching historical places etc. The best time to visit Galena is from May to October.
