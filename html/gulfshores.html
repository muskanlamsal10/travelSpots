Gulf Shores, Alabama






About Gulf Shores



Gulf Shores is a resort city in Baldwin County, Alabama, United States and is known for its pristine white beaches and soothing warm water. The city was little known to the outside world until the destruction took place in 1979 by Hurricane Frederic which contributed to its rebirth as a beautiful tourist destination. The structure of condominiums, beach homes, greens, and gated communities has prompted the Alabama Gulf's prospering place to get-away for families and seasonal residents the same. The best time of the year to visit is from March through May and September through November. It is the perfect place for a fun, relaxing, and unforgettable family beach trip.
