Maui, Hawaii






About Maui



Maui is a Hawaiian island full of nature, Hawaiian tradition, and aloha spirit. It is famous worldwide for its geographical beauty, beaches and breathtaking sights that drags millions of visitors every year. It is located in Maui County, Hawaii. It is the second largest Hawaiian Island that offers natural landscapes such as beaches, volcanoes, palm trees, waterfalls, whale watching and marine ecosystems. For vacation lovers, spring and fall provides the best weather as this time you can also avoid the heavy crowd. It is safe for visitors and April through May and September through November can be the best time to visit Maui.
