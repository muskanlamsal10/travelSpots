Sedona, Arizona






About Sedona



Sedona is surrounded by red-rock buttes, steep canyon walls and pine forests and is an desert town near Flagstaff in Arizona. Sedona is known for two special reasons: The majestic red rock scenery and evergreen vegetation. The Grand Canyon, one of the world's seven wonders is 117 miles from Sedona which is a bonus visit of the most scenic view that one can experience. The best time of the year to visit Sedona is from March to May. It's one of the perfect destination for Hikers. Due to the its outdoor attractions and cultural treasures, it is a go-to place for family vacation.
