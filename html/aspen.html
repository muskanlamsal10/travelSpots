Aspen, Colorado






About Aspen



Aspen, Colorado is located in the Rocky Mountains at around 8,000 feet, is a ski resort town and all year destination for outdoor enjoyment. Aspen was originally named Ute city and is a former mining town that was founded by miners in 1879. Aspen Mountain, Aspen Highlands, Snow-mass and Buttermilk are the four ski areas that makes Aspen so popular and people from all over the world travel to ski here. It is the perfect destination for ski lovers. Snowshoeing and Ice skating are two other things that one can enjoy here if skiing isn't a preference. Aspen sparkles when it's buried under fresh powder and the best time to visit this destination is from December to February.
